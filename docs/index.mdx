Mise is an observability platform built for teams running voice AI at scale. Instead of scanning transcripts or listening through hours of recordings to find what went wrong, you search your entire call corpus in natural language. Mise indexes the acoustic depth of every conversation — sentiment, prosody, interruptions, tension — not just the words.

## What Mise does differently

Most observability tools index by time series or call graph. Mise indexes by **turn and corpus** — the natural unit of a voice conversation. Every turn carries its acoustic fingerprint: not just what was said, but how it was said.
